How watering dates are calculated
The app estimates soil moisture % from the day you last watered, depleting daily using a simplified Hargreaves-Samani evapotranspiration (ET₀) formula that accounts for:

• Temperature — higher temps = faster soil drying
• Humidity — high humidity slows evaporation
• Precipitation — rain events replenish soil moisture
• Soil type — clay retains moisture; sandy drains fast
• Mulch — reduces surface evaporation significantly
• Gallons applied — more water = longer moisture window
• Plant crop coefficient (Kc) — drought-adapted plants tolerate lower moisture before needing water

Watering is recommended when estimated soil moisture drops below the plant's threshold. Your logged history at similar temperatures is always used when available.
